RAMESH: How often should I come back?

DR. REDDY: Annually. Every diabetic patient needs a dilated eye exam once a year, regardless of how well controlled they are. If your diabetes stays in remission range, the risk drops significantly, but we still screen. I'll send my report to Dr. Sharma.
